<span style="text-shadow: 2px 2px 20px white; color:white; font-size: 90%;">'''Items'''</span> are a core part of [[99 Nights in the Forest]]. These are usable items that players can consume as food, use as crafting materials, or burn as fuel for the [[Campfire]]. There are also tools and weapons, which are used to gather resources and fight [[Entities]] like [[Wolf|Wolves]], [[Alpha Wolf|Alpha Wolves]], [[Cultist|Cultists]] and many more. In the [[Update Log|Halloween Update]], you could get few new items through [[Trick or Treating|Trick-or-Treating]], and buying from the new shop. During the update party, you can get admin items that are usually unobtainable in the game.

== Trivia ==
* Even when the player has the '''Vampire Cloak''' equipped, the player can still equip the '''Iron Body''' armor which may have more protection than the cloak but doesn't have the special ability the cloak has.
* A total of 3 items use the '''Raygun''' texture, being the '''Raygun''' itself, the '''Friendly Gun''' and the '''Admin Gun''', making it the item texture with the most uses on different items, aside from Blueprints.
* There are only 4 types of Alien Technology related Items (excluding admin Items) being the '''Raygun''', '''Laser Sword''', '''Laser Cannon''' and the '''Alien Armor'''.
* Interestingly, Obsidiron Boots are crafted on the Meteor Anvil, but cannot be obtained from Obsidiron Chests.
* The amount of hunger '''Carrots''' and '''Berries''' provide are slightly influenced by their size — Carrots and Berries can be larger and smaller which fills up the hunger bar higher or lower depending on its size.
* '''Sacks,''' '''Axes, Rods''' and '''Flutes''' have Old, Good and Strong versions of themselves. However, '''Flashlights''' only have Old and Strong versions — There is no Good Flashlight in the game.
* The '''Air Rifle''' is a ranged weapon that deals the lowest damage in the game out of all weapons. Upon firing, it deals 1 damage. 
* The '''Infernal Sack''' is the only Sack which provides a special effect when an item is placed in it, i.e, when meat is placed inside the Sack, it automatically becomes cooked.
* The [[Cultist|Cultists]] use '''Old Axes''', '''Spears''', '''Morningstars''' and '''Crossbows''' as their weapons. Up until the [[Update Log|Volcanic Biome Part 2 Update]], Crossbows were an unobtainable weapon and only Cultists had it.
* The developers of [[99 Nights in the Forest: Game|99 Nights in the Forest]] made a giant version of the '''Bolt''' and the '''Log''' called the "'''Super Bolt'''" and the "'''Super Log'''" providing {{Scrap|50|Value=50}}and {{Wood|50|Value=50}} respectively when grinded down in the Crafting Bench. ** Despite the '''Super Log''' being a giant version of the log, it still gives the same amount of fuel as a normal log.
